What

Cloud/SaaS is shared responsibility: providers secure the platform; you secure data, identities, configurations, and lawful processing. Privacy controls must cover data residency, encryption, access, vendor contracts, and cross-border transfers (SCCs).

Why It Matters

Misconfigurations and unchecked vendors cause many incidents. Strong agreements (DPAs), technical controls (customer-managed keys), and regionalization reduce risk and ease compliance.

Example

  • Shopify: Encrypts merchant & customer data in the cloud; merchants configure access and apps carefully.
  • Retail analytics stack: Region-lock PII in the EU; export only anonymized aggregates elsewhere with SCCs.

How (Starter Pack)

  • Enable encryption at rest with KMS/HSM; consider customer-managed keys.
  • Harden identity: SSO, MFA, least privilege, short-lived credentials.
  • Review DPA/SCCs; maintain a vendor register, conduct periodic assessments.
  • Use data residency controls; segregate PII; tokenize across regions.
