Spot privacy failures before they ship. Your users (and regulators) will love you.

What

Privacy threat modeling systematically identifies how a system could enable misuse of personal data (re-identification, excessive collection, undisclosed sharing, etc.). Use frameworks like LINDDUN to structure the hunt.

Why It Matters

Early detection is cheaper than incident response. Modeling clarifies mitigations (minimization, purpose limits, DP, encryption, access control) and informs PIAs and compliance docs.

Example

  • Retail checkout: Map data flows for PII, payment, and logs. Identify over-collection (phone + email + precise location). Reduce to essentials.
  • Loyalty + geolocation: Flag linkability risk; use rotating IDs and coarse-grain location for analytics.

How (Starter Pack)

  • Draw a data flow diagram (DFD) and list assets, actors, stores, and flows.
  • Apply LINDDUN to each flow; capture risks and severities.
  • Pick mitigations and owners; add them to your backlog/PRD.
  • Re-run the model on major changes; link to PIA and security reviews.
