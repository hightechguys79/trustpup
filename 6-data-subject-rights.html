What

DSR are the tools individuals have to control their data: access copies, rectify errors, delete info, port to another service, and sometimes object or restrict processing. Under CCPA/CPRA, Californians can also opt out of the sale/share of their personal info.

Why It Matters

These rights are time-boxed (e.g., ~30 days under GDPR). Smooth, auditable fulfillment builds trust and keeps regulators happy; missed deadlines and sloppy responses do the opposite.

Example

  • Amazon/Walmart: Self-serve portals for access and deletion requests.
  • Spotify (EU): GDPR-compliant data export (playlists, listening history).

How (Starter Pack)
