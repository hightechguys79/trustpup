Privacy-Safe Data Lakes

Sanitized pipelines for ML and analytics

What

Ingestion pipelines that detect, classify, and transform sensitive data before it lands in your lake. Use tokenisation, redaction, anonymisation, and DP for published aggregates.

Why It Matters

Raw dumps of receipts, logs, or chats become re-identification traps. Sanitisation at the edge keeps ML useful and datasets safe.

Example

  • ETL for Receipts & Support Logs: Classifiers catch PII/PHI. Emails → tokens; rare SKUs bucketed; ZIP codes coarsened; counts released with DP.

How (Starter Pack)

  • Run PII scanners with a policy engine (mask/drop/tokenise).
  • Attach schema tags (purpose, retention, lawful basis). Enforce at read/write.
  • Adopt a tokenisation service (format-preserving where needed).
  • Apply k-anonymity/l-diversity/t-closeness guidance; use DP for public stats.
  • Use data contracts to reject non-conformant payloads.
