Federated Analytics

Learn from data without collecting it

What

Analytics and ML where raw data stays on the device or in-region. Models train locally; only updates/gradients are shared. Add Differential Privacy (DP) and secure aggregation to bound individual leakage.

Why It Matters

Gain cross-store/app insights without centralising sensitive events—reducing breach blast radius and simplifying data-locality compliance.

Example

  • Retail Personalisation: On-device learning for next-best offer. Gradients are securely aggregated, with DP noise added before global updates—no keystrokes or raw purchase logs uploaded.

How (Starter Pack)

  • Prototype with TensorFlow Federated or PySyft.
  • Use DP-SGD (TensorFlow Privacy) to track ε/δ and privacy budgets.
  • Enable secure aggregation so servers can’t read a single client update.
  • Document fairness, drift checks, and client participation policies.
