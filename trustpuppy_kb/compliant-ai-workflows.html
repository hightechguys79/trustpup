Compliant AI Workflows

Automation with verifiable decision trails

What

Policy-aware automations that explain decisions, preserve consent receipts, and maintain tamper-evident audit logs (who did what, to which record, under which lawful basis).

Why It Matters

Regulators and customers ask “why?”. Verifiable trails support GDPR accountability, DSR SLAs, internal reviews, and faster incident response.

Example

  • DSR Automation: Intake → ID verify → route to systems → erase/tokenise → confirm. Every step logged with timestamps, policy IDs, and evidence hashes.

How (Starter Pack)

  • Attach policy rules & reason codes to each action (explainability hooks).
  • Append-only logs with cryptographic hashing and time-stamps.
  • Separation of duties (maker/checker) for de-tokenisation or data exports.
  • Track SLAs; auto-escalate exceptions; store DPA/SCC versions in logs.
