Intelligent review systems for sensitive operations

What

Human-in-the-loop designs that pair AI speed with human judgment. The system scores risk, redacts by default, and escalates sensitive cases for one-click approve/deny with instant remediation.

Why It Matters

Some decisions need context and accountability (health, finance, minors). HITL reduces bias, prevents silent failures, and creates defensible outcomes.

Example

  • Refund + Address Change: AI drafts response and flags possible PII exposure. If the request involves unusual refunds or prescription items, route to a trained privacy reviewer; capture rationale.

How (Starter Pack)

  • Risk scoring: data category, volume, jurisdiction, role, anomaly signals.
  • Progressive disclosure: show masked data; unmask only on justified need.
  • Escalation matrix: rules for human approval; capture rationale and overrides.
  • One-click remediation: revoke tokens, rotate keys, trigger erasure jobs.
