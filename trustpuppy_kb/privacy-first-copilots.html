Custom AI assistants with built-in privacy controls

What

AI copilots that answer enterprise questions without leaking sensitive fields. They enforce field-level masking, consent checks, policy-aware retrieval, and full auditability. Prompts and responses are scrubbed, and every access is logged with purpose and actor.

Why It Matters

LLM prompts can accidentally include PII/PHI or trade secrets. Privacy-first controls prevent over-collection, purpose creep, and prompt exfiltration while creating defensible audit trails.

Example

  • Customer Care Copilot: Sees order totals and line items but masks card PAN and full address unless the role is ‚ÄúRefund Ops‚Äù with active consent.
  • Pharmacy: Can cite formulary guidance, but cannot surface prescription history unless HIPAA role + break-glass reason is provided and logged.

How (Starter Pack)

  • Classify columns (PII/sensitive) and tag with policies (mask, tokenize, deny).
  • Policy-aware RAG so retrieval filters snippets by role, purpose, and consent.
  • Scrub prompts/responses for high-risk entities (SSN, PAN, diagnosis codes).
  • Bind answers to lawful basis & consent receipt; emit immutable, signed logs.
